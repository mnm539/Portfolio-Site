Madeline McKernan

Madeline McKernan is currently a Convergence Journalism and Political Science Major at the University of Missouri. She has specialized skills in reporting, video production, writing, social media and photography and experience in marketing and communications. She also has hands-on newsroom experience at KOMU 8 News, Vox Magazine and The Coulumbia Missourian.

McKernan currently works as a Research Assistant at the Entrepreneurship Legal Clinic at the Missouri School of Law. She is gaining valuable exposure to Intellectual Property law, as well as learning how legal clinics are run. She has had an in-depth look at contract writing and how Trademarks are researched and filed through the USPTO. She has attended webinars regarding USPTO Trademark clinics and the General Data Protection Regulation.

McKernan previously served as a summer Marketing and Communications intern for The Heart of Missouri United Way. She gained experience in public relations, newsletter writing and learned how to create marketing materials.

McKernan was a member of the University of Missouri Swimming and Diving Team, and she competed as a Division 1 athlete form 2014-2018. In 2016, she won NCAA All-American acolades at the National meet on platform, and was the first Mizzou diver to place in the top 8 in the platform event. In 2018, she won All-SEC accolades after placing 3rd at the SEC Conference meet. McKernan was a 3-time NCAA Team member and 4-time SEC Team member. All four years on the Swimming and Diving team she recieved SEC Honor Roll recognition.
